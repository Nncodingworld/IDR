Academic Insight + Real World Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

Through partnerships with leading UK universities and global industry experts, IDR bridges academic research with real-world industry challenges, developing professionals capable of managing complex digital risk environments.

Our Services
